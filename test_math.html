Introduction

This document tests various LaTeX math formulas using the MD to PDF converter.

Basic Math Operations

  • Addition: \(a + b = c\)
  • Subtraction: \(x - y = z\)
  • Multiplication: \(m \times n = p\) or \(m \cdot n = p\)
  • Division: \(\frac{a}{b} = c\) or \(a/b = c\)

Powers and Roots

  • Square: \(x^2\)
  • Cube: \(x^3\)
  • nth power: \(x^n\)
  • Square root: \(\sqrt{x}\)
  • nth root: \(\sqrt[n]{x}\)
  • Complex: \(\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}\)

Trigonometry

  • Sine: \(\sin(\theta)\)
  • Cosine: \(\cos(\theta)\)
  • Tangent: \(\tan(\theta)\)
  • Inverse: \(\sin^{-1}(x)\) or \(\arcsin(x)\)
  • Pythagorean identity: \(\sin^2(x) + \cos^2(x) = 1\)

Einstein’s Equation

\[E = mc^2\]

Quadratic Formula

\[x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\]

Integral

\[\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}\]

Summation

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}\]

Product

\[\prod_{i=1}^{n} i = n!\]

Matrix Operations

Simple Matrix

\[\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix}\]

Identity Matrix

\[I = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\]

Matrix Multiplication

\[\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \times \begin{pmatrix} e & f \\ g & h \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} ae + bg & af + bh \\ ce + dg & cf + dh \end{pmatrix}\]

Calculus

Derivative

  • Simple derivative: \(\frac{d}{dx}x^2 = 2x\)
  • Partial derivative: \(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\)
  • Chain rule: \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \cdot \frac{du}{dx}\)

Integral Forms

\[\int x^2 dx = \frac{x^3}{3} + C\]

\[\int_{a}^{b} f(x) dx = F(b) - F(a)\]

\[\iint_D f(x,y) dA\]

Limits

  • Simple limit: \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1\)
  • One-sided limit: \(\lim_{x \to 0^+} f(x)\)
  • Infinity limit: \(\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} = 0\)

Complex Numbers

  • Imaginary unit: \(i^2 = -1\) or \(j^2 = -1\)
  • Complex number: \(z = a + bi\)
  • Complex conjugate: \(\overline{z} = a - bi\)
  • Magnitude: \(|z| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}\)

Set Theory

  • Element of: \(x \in A\)
  • Not element of: \(x \notin A\)
  • Subset: \(A \subset B\) or \(A \subseteq B\)
  • Union: \(A \cup B\)
  • Intersection: \(A \cap B\)
  • Empty set: \(\emptyset\)
  • Universal set: \(\mathbb{U}\)

Logic

  • And: \(\land\) or \(\wedge\)
  • Or: \(\lor\) or \(\vee\)
  • Not: \(\neg\) or \(\lnot\)
  • Implies: \(\implies\) or \(\rightarrow\)
  • Equivalent: \(\iff\) or \(\leftrightarrow\)
  • For all: \(\forall\)
  • Exists: \(\exists\)
